# Creator Style Map

## MoneyXYZ

Signals:
- financial cognition
- strong hook based on tension or reversal
- book/reference authority
- point 1/2/3 logic blocks
- emotional release near the end
- clear but restrained CTA

Use when the video feels like:
- cognitive upgrade through wealth/decision framing
- structured explanatory storytelling

## Dan Koe

Signals:
- creator economy / one-person business / self-development
- identity transformation language
- philosophy + systems + action loop
- broad but elegant abstractions
- turns ideas into frameworks

Use when the video feels like:
- teaching worldview + system + self-direction

## 認知便利店M

Signals:
- business psychology / thinking models / commercial cases
- visually packable insights
- short-form punchy reframes
- practical but concept-led
- often highly memetic and compressible

Use when the video feels like:
- a concept convenience store for fast cognitive upgrades

## Hybrid outputs

When the video mixes styles, say so explicitly:
- "MoneyXYZ shell, Dan Koe core"
- "Dan Koe worldview with 認知便利店 packaging"
- "認知便利店 topic selection with MoneyXYZ structure"
